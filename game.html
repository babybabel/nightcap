As a sandman of the world between the eyes and brain, Whiskey Nightcap has many unique abilities! Despite being light and floaty, he can pack a punch using his large lantern to hit and fire at the enemies he will face.

He isn't invincible, however, and will have to avoid getting hurt, or else his Lunar Phase will slow. The faster Whiskey's lunar phases advance, the more powerful he is; but if they slow to a halt, he will be ejected from the dream.
