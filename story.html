The world of the waking is in danger!

As a Sandman, it's Whiskey Nightcap's job to enter the dreams of the unconscious, securing important ideas and memories within their minds. The balance between the waking and sleeping world rests upon this, with the dreams that Whiskey collects being fed to Angels.

If the dreams stop coming and the Angels get hungry, our custodian must do whatever it takes to snuff out the nightmares and save the minds of the resting.

Whiskey roaming the halls of the Adumbral Abbey, the hidden world where he embarks on his adventures and where the Angels rest
